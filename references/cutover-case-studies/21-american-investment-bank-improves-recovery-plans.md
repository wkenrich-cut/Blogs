# American Investment Bank Improves Recovery Plans with Cutover

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/american-investment-bank-improves-operational-resilience

---

## Overview

An American investment bank replaced an inadequate home-grown resilience system with Cutover, enabling thousands of Technical Recovery Plans (TRPs) to be configured into test scenarios in minutes rather than weeks, and providing the visibility and orchestration needed for regulatory compliance.

---

## The Challenge: Implementing a Comprehensive Resilience Solution

Like other financial services firms, the American investment bank had an obligation to demonstrate their ability to deal with a catastrophic scenario in a timely manner. They needed the capability to pull together thousands of standardized Technical Recovery Plans (TRPs) into **test scenarios in minutes rather than weeks**. The operational resilience testing function involved thousands of people globally and up to 2,000 applications tested every year.

The existing home-grown system was inadequate and did not provide the level of planning, visibility, communication, orchestration, or observability needed for regulatory requirements.

---

## The Solution: Comprehensive Operational Resilience Platform

With Cutover, a single data center recovery (DCR) test encompassing more than **300 TRPs** can be prepared and managed independently, then merged into Cutover for orchestration and enterprise observability.

Having TRPs in Cutover allowed the bank to standardize them using templates, minimizing the work to finalize DCR test runbooks. Cutover provides users with status information and updates during the test without manual effort.

The bank also created templated TRPs in Cutover, making it quicker and easier for users to find, review, edit, and execute TRPs and build new ones.

### Key Improvements

- Better compliance with demonstrable evidence of testing and associated timings
- Auto-calculation of recovery time objective through structured TRPs
- Ability to benchmark performance in data center tests against previous runs
- Integration with existing apps to provide observability across the entire process

---

## The Outcome

The bank is now in the process of decommissioning its existing system in favor of Cutover. The team running events is now better informed during data center tests, improving decision-making and collaboration with auditors. Due to the success of resilience activities, Cutover is also being used to support building power-downs and orchestrate infrastructure parts of resilience testing.

---

## Key Results

- Thousands of TRPs configured into test scenarios in minutes
- 2,000 applications covered in annual resilience testing
- 300+ TRPs per DCR test orchestrated in Cutover
- Full regulatory compliance with auto-generated audit evidence
- Legacy home-grown system being decommissioned
