# A Global Bank's Self-Guided Journey to the Cloud

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/global-banks-self-guided-journey-to-the-cloud

---

## Overview

A global bank (one of the world's largest) used Cutover to standardize and accelerate its migration to AWS, moving applications **three times faster** and achieving 100% regulatory compliance with an automated audit trail.

---

## The Challenge: A Disconnected Cloud Migration Team

The bank's move to AWS brought together 35 people from traditional infrastructure backgrounds spanning databases, networks, storage, and visualization — scattered across four countries and different departments. The challenge was ensuring everyone was aligned and working in a multi-disciplined, cross-team approach. A key concern remained around regulatory sign-off to keep driving migrations forward.

It was clear from the start that spreadsheets, email chains, and single-threaded project management solutions were not the answer. They needed a tool that would provide visibility across the board, set guardrails for delivery, and create an audit trail for compliance.

---

## The Solution: A Standardized and Repeatable Cloud Migration Process

Using Cutover, the bank standardized its cloud migrations and created a repeatable, scalable process by:

- **Establishing a guided migration path with guardrails.** With templated runbooks, they created a consistent path to production for application teams and set guardrails to enable control.
- **Completing the AWS maturity assessment in-house.** They mapped the organization's maturity through migration stages with a 180-step runbook providing the necessary answers to complete the assessment internally.
- **Validating automations to make the process faster.** Integrating with the service catalog enabled them to achieve key milestones faster and eliminate human error.

---

## The Results: Three Times Faster Migration

- **Migrating three times faster with better team orchestration.** The bank tracked the progress of the central cloud program and all lines of business, with every member of the team understanding when each task started and ended.
- **Achieving 100% compliance with an automated audit trail.** The risk and regulatory compliance assessment was successfully completed using Cutover with full visibility across the organization.
- **A massively reduced decommissioning timeframe.** A repeatable Cutover template considerably reduced the risks associated with prematurely decommissioning applications.
- Up to **50% reduction in planning and auditing time** for some tasks.
