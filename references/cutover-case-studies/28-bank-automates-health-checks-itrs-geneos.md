# Major American Bank Uses ITRS Geneos Integration with Cutover to Automate System Health Checks

## Overview

A major American bank's Markets Team used Cutover's ITRS Geneos integration to automate daily system health checks, reducing required staff from 5 to 2 people, cutting execution time by 90%, and increasing monitoring capacity fivefold.

---

## The Challenge: Manual System Health Checks

The Markets Team at a Major American Bank had to conduct daily health checks to ensure all applications, hardware, software, databases, and middleware for their markets automatic payments services were set up before trading. Before Cutover, this was a highly manual process that took a long time. If there was a problem, the team had to manually look through individual logs, making incident response time slow. Failure to ensure everything was set up correctly in time would negatively impact trading.

---

## The Solution: Automated System Health Checks via Integration

The team started using Cutover to manage these daily health checks via an integration with their monitoring tool **ITRS Geneos**. This removed the need for lots of people to complete a big list of manual tasks — instead, fewer people were needed just to kick off and oversee the hundreds of checks being carried out, with real-time status updates provided via dashboards.

The integration cut down incident response time as, rather than looking through logs one at a time, the team could immediately see via Cutover where failures had occurred and where their focus needed to be.

---

## Key Benefits

- **Staff reduced from 5 to 2 people**, freeing up capacity for strategic activities
- **Monitoring capacity increased from dozens to hundreds of checks**, enabling an expansion in the type of checks performed
- **Core environment restarts reduced from 3.5 hours each week to just 5 minutes**
- **Increased incident prevention** — more checks being carried out means incidents that would have gone unnoticed are now being prevented
- **Decreased resolution time** — incidents are discovered quicker, giving more time to meet resolution SLAs

---

## The Outcome: Quadrupled Efficiency

As a result of the automation and visibility provided by Cutover, the team has **quadrupled its efficiency** and reduced resources required for health checks by **60%**. Monitoring capacity has increased **fivefold** and they have saved over **90% of their execution time**, enabling them to execute **30,000 tasks in the last quarter**.
