# Improved Business Continuity Planning and Execution for Leading Investment Firm

## Overview

A global alternative investment management firm managing $40+ billion in assets used Cutover to improve its Business Continuity Planning, successfully executing a global BCP test even when the Implementation Manager was hospitalized mid-event.

---

## The Challenge: A Better Way to Manage Business Continuity Planning

A global alternative investment management firm, which manages more than **$40 billion in assets**, needed a better way to manage its Business Continuity Planning (BCP) and execution. The firm conducts **four global BCP tests a year**, each one involving over 50 resources dispersed around the world.

Cutover was chosen as the solution because it creates an "operational theater" that enables status monitoring and full visualization, elevates virtual team collaboration, and forms a system of record throughout the implementation event.

---

## The Solution: Greater Flexibility and Insurance Against Failure

When the firm initially began using Cutover, they imported existing planning spreadsheets. However, they quickly became proficient at building plans directly in Cutover and no longer use spreadsheets in this capacity.

Cutover provided greater flexibility and insurance against unexpected changes. For the very first BCP test in Cutover, **the Implementation Manager was rushed into hospital due to a medical emergency**. Previously, the team would have had to cancel the event — but they decided to continue with the help of the SaaS-based platform due to its accessibility and visibility. The event was executed successfully and the Implementation Manager was able to **monitor progress from his hospital bed on his cell phone**.

### Benefits Delivered

- Business continuity event plan invocation
- Reduced risk relating to the invocation and control of a BCP event plan
- Enhanced communication and improved collaboration around the execution of BCP events

---

## The Outcome: Continued Expansion into New Use Cases

After a year of using Cutover for BCP test planning and execution, the firm began using Cutover for other diverse use cases including a major office move.

---

## Key Results

- 4 global BCP tests per year managed in Cutover
- 50+ globally dispersed resources coordinated per test
- BCP event completed despite key manager being hospitalized
- Spreadsheets fully replaced with Cutover runbooks
- Expanded to non-BCP use cases (major office move)
