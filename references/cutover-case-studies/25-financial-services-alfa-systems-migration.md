# Financial Services Company Successfully Orchestrates Move to Alfa Systems Asset Finance Platform

---

## Overview

One of the largest car finance companies in the US used Cutover to orchestrate a multi-year migration from a legacy mainframe to the Alfa Systems cloud platform, onboarding nearly 1,000 people and successfully delivering the program's most complex phase.

---

## The Challenge: Transfer Asset Financing to a New Cloud-Based Platform

A financial services company, and one of the largest car finance companies in the US, needed to move from an old mainframe managing their asset financing to a new cloud-based Alfa Systems digital platform. This was a multi-year program and the biggest one the company had ever undertaken, involving the part of the company responsible for **80% of revenue**. Because this was a major program they would spend years and millions of dollars delivering, failure was not an option.

They realized early on they needed a solution to help manage the migration — a better way of working that did not involve a war room with charts and people being orchestrated manually.

---

## The Solution: A Better Way to Orchestrate the Entire Event

In a short space of time, **nearly 1,000 people** in the company were onboarded and trained and were able to manage the process themselves very quickly. They rapidly created their plans in Cutover and were able to run full dress rehearsals before the event to ensure everything was correct.

Cutover provided the ability to visualize the dependency management tree with a critical path, and delivered real-time telemetry. Clear and comprehensive dashboards showed where they were and what they needed to do.

The automated communications in the Cutover platform reduced the administrative burden for the command and control team. Cutover's automatic system of record also created an indelible audit trail, ensuring all aspects of the event were accurately recorded without any post-event work.

---

## The Outcome: Successfully Delivered Change

The financial services company could not have delivered the amount of change they did without Cutover. Going forward, Cutover will be used for all future critical change programs.
