# Furniture Retailer Updates Global Infrastructure

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/furniture-retailer-updates-global-infrastructure

---

## Overview

A major furniture retailer used Cutover during a multi-year program to update global e-commerce, warehouse, and stock infrastructure, executing over 300 runbooks across 57 countries with zero rollbacks.

---

## The Challenge: Eliminating Risk During a Multi-Year Program

A furniture retailer began a multi-year program to update global e-commerce, warehouse, and stock infrastructure to meet the growing demand for online shopping, exacerbated by COVID-19. They needed to eliminate the risk of system outages during critical and frequent software updates which, if they went wrong, could significantly impact revenues and reputation.

Preparations for each software release or update event took multiple weeks when it needed to be done in days or hours, and there was a high level of risk during live operations.

---

## The Solution: Simulation and Continuous Improvement

Cutover enabled collaboration across a vast number of global teams and gave them the ability to simulate and improve live implementation plans before the actual go live. During the live implementations they also had real-time visibility of progress.

---

## Results

After the adoption of Cutover, the preparation time and outage time were significantly reduced, meeting the company's goals for the program. They were able to execute more than **300 runbooks across 57 countries without a single rollback**.

---

## Key Results

- 300+ runbooks executed across 57 countries
- Zero rollbacks across the entire program
- Preparation time significantly reduced
- Outage time significantly reduced
- Real-time visibility during live implementations
