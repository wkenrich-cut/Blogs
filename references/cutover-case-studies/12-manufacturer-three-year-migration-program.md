# Manufacturer Uses Cutover to Manage Its Three-Year Migration Program

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/manufacturer-uses-cutover-three-year-migration-program

---

## Overview

A British-run US engineering and manufacturing company used Cutover to manage complex, multi-region migration programs — replacing spreadsheets and emails with a single collaborative platform that gave global teams and senior leadership real-time visibility.

---

## The Challenge: Spreadsheets Can't Handle the Complexity of Migration Programs

Senior leadership at a British-run US company that specializes in the engineering and manufacturing of protection equipment felt that the program of migrations they had planned over the next three years were too risky to be done with their standard tooling of spreadsheets, emails, and project management tools. They felt that due to the scale and complexity of the program, orchestration was going to be close to impossible with these existing tools.

They needed a more comprehensive solution to give them visibility of the live event and help them to plan, orchestrate, and analyze the migrations from start to finish. Visibility and communication were especially important because they had remote teams all over the world working together.

---

## The Solution: Cutover Enabled Teams to Work Collaboratively

The manufacturer used Cutover to carry out the first of these migrations, updating the payroll system. Teams from across multiple regions including Brazil, Italy, the US, and the UK were involved in the migration and were able to work collaboratively through Cutover. The senior stakeholders were able to keep track of how the migration was going in real time and quickly make decisions based on accurate data.

During their first major migration using Cutover, the leadership team put their full trust in the platform and completely retired spreadsheets and other manual tools. Cutover was also used to coordinate third parties involved, providing a single platform that aligned everyone globally.

The **node map feature** clearly visualizes the flow of work as a networked set of processes, which helps to identify parallel tasks and dependencies, manage the critical path, and stay on schedule. This enabled those running the migrations to identify a roadblock mid-event and rectify it in real time.

The built-in communications in Cutover also enabled the team to send automated communications every two hours to keep everyone in the know. This freed up those doing the work from updating others and meant the CIO and CFO didn't have to ask for updates.

---

## Key Results

- Spreadsheets and manual tools fully retired
- Global teams across Brazil, Italy, US, and UK coordinated in one platform
- Real-time decision-making enabled for CIO and CFO
- Mid-event roadblocks identified and resolved using node map
- Automated stakeholder communications sent every two hours
