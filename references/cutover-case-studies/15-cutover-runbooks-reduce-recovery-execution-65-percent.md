# Cutover Runbooks Make Failovers Simple and Reduce Recovery Execution by 65%

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/cutover-runbooks-make-failovers-simple-reduce-post-event-review-from-hours-to-minutes

---

## Overview

A financial services company replaced highly manual, uncoordinated failover procedures with Cutover automated runbooks, reducing recovery execution and verification time by 65% and cutting post-event reporting from 3–4 hours to just 5–10 minutes.

---

## The Problem: Uncoordinated and Manual Failover Procedures

A financial services company had to perform data center disaster recovery failover procedures every six months involving around 50 applications that supported their loans and fees services. They were performing simulated failovers to test this around once a week but were not always consistent. The highly manual failover simulation process involved over 14 teams that needed to be coordinated for successful failover recovery.

The process and execution tools were separate — information was held in Confluence while steps were completed using various software tools such as Jenkins and Rundeck. The Application Owner then had to set up a bridge call with the Application Team and spend three to four hours running the plan, during which time everyone involved had to be on the call and couldn't work on anything else.

Post-event reporting was also highly manual and time-consuming, taking a further three to four hours after the test was complete with a high risk of human error.

---

## The Solution: Automated Runbooks and Comprehensive Dashboards

Cutover now acts as the central planning and execution hub for simulated and real data center failovers. After building their failover recovery plans in Cutover's automated runbooks, the Application Team can kick off an automated runbook, and the people involved can work on other things and are notified when they need to log in and complete their tasks.

Thanks to integrations with Jenkins and Rundeck, all the information in those systems such as code and scripts is available in the Cutover runbook. The team also integrated Cutover with Microsoft Teams to further improve communication and visibility.

Cutover's dashboards provided huge value for measuring recovery time objectives (RTOs) against recovery time actuals (RTAs). Post-event, the Application Owner can now download the task list from Cutover and import the files straight into Fusion, reducing post-event reporting from a **three to four-hour process to five to ten minutes**.

> "The ability to leave comments in a centralized place and access performance metrics post-event has changed the game for our post-mortem activities and preparations for the next event." — Application Owner

---

## The Outcome: 65% Faster Recovery Execution and Verification

Many of the activities for the failovers were run by scripts with some manual intervention. Cutover enabled the Application Team to integrate with those scripts to mature them past manual intervention, **reducing recovery execution and verification time by 65%**.

The Application Team estimated that Cutover is ten times better than their previous manual way of working.
