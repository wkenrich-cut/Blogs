# Danske Bank Improves Resilience Efficiency by 300%

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/danske-bank-improves-resilience-efficiency-300

---

## Overview

Danske Bank, Denmark's largest bank, transformed its IT resilience operations using Cutover, going from testing 130 services in 10 hours to flipping over 400 services in 14 hours — a 300% efficiency improvement.

---

## Background

Danske Bank is a Danish multinational banking and financial services corporation with over 21,000 employees. Headquartered in Copenhagen, it is the largest bank in Denmark and a major retail bank in the northern European region, serving 3.3 million personal and business customers and more than 2,300 corporate and institutional customers in 8 countries.

Danske Bank's IT resilience team wanted a better way to manage its resilience activities. Like every major bank, it was seeing increasing regulatory scrutiny and needed to reduce the risk of incidents that could impact customers. The existing processes were effective but inefficient and manually intensive.

The first step on this journey was improving the performance of the bank's Data Center Recovery testing, which was mainly being run using spreadsheets and Confluence. Using these methods, they were only able to test and shut down around **130 services in 10 hours**, which was neither fast nor efficient enough.

---

## The Solution: Improving Resilience with Cutover

The IT Resilience team was introduced to Cutover via a partner and, after evaluating Cutover against other suppliers, they decided to onboard Cutover for Data Center Recovery testing.

Using Cutover, the team orchestrated two large tests:

1. For the first test, they converted existing failover plans into a Cutover runbook containing **3,000 tasks**.
2. For the second test, they uploaded individual plans for each database service owner, who could check on the progress of their team leading up to the event. On the day of the event, the resilience team merged all of these plans into a large runbook so they could manage the event as a whole.

### Key Cutover Capabilities Leveraged

**Dependency mapping and node map view:** Being able to map dependencies and see them on the node map view allowed the resilience managers to get a clear view of the entire process from start to finish.

**Rehearsing resilience tests:** Following training sessions with Cutover, the team went through rehearsal runs, identifying any changes that needed to be made, which enabled a smoother go live.

**Operational resilience dashboards:** Dashboards provided real-time updates for all participants. Milestone tasks provided a quick overview of each important resilience stage and what tasks were in progress or ready to be executed.

**Audit:** Easy access and downloadable audits allowed the team to gather fine details on the timings of every task in the event as part of the post-event reporting and for evidential purposes.

---

## The Outcome: 300% Improvement in Resilience Efficiency

Since they started using Cutover in 2019, Danske Bank has implemented Cutover across the organization and can now flip over **400 services in 14 hours — a 300% efficiency improvement** over their previous methods.
