# Major American Bank Orchestrates Largest Data Center Isolation Event

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/major-american-bank-orchestrates-largest-data-center-isolation-event

---

## Overview

A major American bank used Cutover to orchestrate a 16-hour data center failover event involving 2,500 people and 863 critical applications across 13 lines of business, completing flawlessly.

---

## The Challenge: Testing Data Center Failover at Scale

A major American bank wanted to test that they could successfully execute a major data center failover event and get all of their **863 critical applications** back online within a set time — and credibly attest to this capability to the regulator.

Around 2,500 people at the bank were involved in the process, with **1,863 of them executing tasks** in Cutover automated runbooks. Cutover's Client Success Team conducted 25 hours of training over two weeks to ensure preparation for the event.

---

## The Solution: Orchestrated Data Center Failover

### Preparation

- Every participant could be contacted via SMS through Cutover, meaning they didn't have to stay on an open bridge for the entire 16-hour event — they could step away and were notified when needed.
- Each task had three contacts assigned: if the primary person was unavailable, secondary and tertiary contacts were ready in the system — no need to search directories or spreadsheets.
- The bank built runbooks for the failover covering 863 applications and 13 different lines of business.
- Each line of business had its own dashboard to easily filter for relevant tasks and information.

### During the Event

- Cutover enabled users to quickly filter the runbook to find relevant tasks, saving time and ensuring nothing was missed.
- 13 business-unit dashboards provided real-time visibility for each team.

---

## The Outcome: Flawless Execution

In total, the event took **16 hours and 22 minutes** to complete. The Command Center team felt everything went flawlessly. Any minor hiccups with users were swiftly resolved and despite it being a complex and high-risk operation, **all 863 applications were successfully failed over**.

The team is already using Cutover to analyze and plan for the next event, using the insights provided by the platform to improve for next time.

> "Using Cutover, we are now able to easily orchestrate large events like this one. The platform also helps us gain insights and find areas for improvement for future events." — Command Center
