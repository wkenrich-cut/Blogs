# Investment Bank Reduces IT DR Planning Time by 70%

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/american-investment-bank-operational-resilience

---

## Overview

An American investment bank used Cutover to transform its disaster recovery testing, reducing event planning time by 70% and successfully executing 143,000 tasks across 10,000 users.

---

## The Need: Reducing the Time to Test Thousands of Disaster Recovery Plans

Like other financial services firms, the American bank had an obligation to demonstrate their ability to deal with a catastrophic scenario in a timely manner. They needed the capability to pull together thousands of standardized technical disaster recovery plans into **test scenarios in minutes rather than weeks**. The resilience testing function involved thousands of people globally and up to 2,000 applications that needed to be tested every year.

The existing home-grown system for this process was inadequate and did not provide the level of IT disaster recovery planning, visibility, communication, orchestration, or observability that was needed. It lacked the required level of resilience assurance needed to meet regulatory requirements.

---

## The Solution: A Single Data Recovery Test for Improved Disaster Recovery

Cutover's SaaS platform and automated runbooks provided an effective disaster recovery planning solution. A single data center recovery (DCR) test that could encompass more than 300 bank disaster recovery plans could be prepared and managed independently, then merged into Cutover for orchestration and enterprise observability.

Having the bank's disaster recovery plans in Cutover automated runbooks also allowed them to standardize with templates, minimizing the work to finalize DCR test runbooks.

### Additional Improvements

- Auto-calculation of recovery time objective through structured disaster recovery plans
- Standardized and observable event execution led to fewer issues and better decision-making
- Ability to benchmark performance in data center tests against previous runs
- Integration with existing apps to provide observability across the entire process
- Better compliance with demonstrable evidence of testing and associated timings
- Robust auditability and reporting meeting all audit requirements

---

## Results: Better Informed, Faster Disaster Recovery

Using Cutover, the bank was able to **reduce event planning time by 70%** and easily facilitated and recorded all **143,000 completed tasks across its 10,000 users**.

The bank is now in the process of decommissioning its existing system in favor of Cutover. The team running the event is now better informed during data center tests, helping to improve decision-making and collaborate with auditors.
