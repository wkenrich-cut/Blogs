# American Bank Improves Month-End Closes with Cutover

**Source:** https://cutover.com/success-stories/american-bank-replaces-spreadsheets-with-cutover-for-month-end-closes

---

## Overview

A major American bank's finance team replaced its legacy internal tool with Cutover to manage month-end closes, giving 170 users better visibility, accountability, and repeatable processes.

---

## The Problem: Poor Visibility and Accountability of Month-End Processes

The finance team in a major American bank needed a better way to manage month-end closes. They were using an in-house tool for these processes, which offered little more functionality than a spreadsheet. It was used to assign groups and teams specific tasks but there was no visibility of progress or whether tasks had been completed and at what time.

This process included registering expenses, checking who paid, recording what went in and out, and tying up financials to ensure the books were balanced. Because of the lack of functionality, completing these processes could take a few days of team members going through journal entries and checking balance sheets against receipts and files.

---

## The Solution: Cutover Runbooks for Month-End Closes

The team adopted Cutover as the tool for managing month-end closes. They built linear runbooks, and users could mark their tasks as completed, with overall progress viewable via dashboards.

**170 users** migrated from the old systems onto Cutover, and are now using the platform at the end of every month. This requires minimal effort as they have created a standardized runbook template within Cutover to clone and use every time, making the process easily repeatable.

Cutover's audit trail and dashboard ensured both internal and external visibility and reporting.

---

## The Outcome: Time Saved on Month-End Closes

The move from internal tooling and spreadsheets was seamless and provided the team with much more functionality to ensure their month-end closes went smoothly. The team particularly benefitted from the repeatability and time savings thanks to runbook templates that could be reused every month.

> "The rollover process from our internal tool to Cutover couldn't have been smoother."

---

## Key Results

- 170 users migrated from legacy system to Cutover
- Standardized runbook template cloned monthly
- Full audit trail for internal and external reporting
- Multi-day close processes streamlined
- Repeatable, scalable monthly process established
