# **Content Type Rules**

---

## **1. Blog Posts (SEO + Thought Leadership Hybrid)**

### **Before Writing (If Missing — Ask User):**

* Primary keyword
* Secondary keywords
* CTA
* Topic / angle

---

### **Structure (Mandatory)**

```
# H1 (Primary keyword)

Intro (2–4 sentences, hook + keyword + internal link)

## Section 1
## Section 2
## Section 3 (3–6 sections total)

### Frequently Asked Questions

## CTA Section
```

---

### **SEO Rules**

* Primary keyword → 6–8 times
* Secondary keywords → 1–2 times each
* Internal link → within first 200 words
* Word count → 700–1200 (ideal ~900)
* Bullet list OR numbered list required
* FAQ (3–6 questions)
* CTA always at end

---

### **Tone for Blogs**

Blend:

* **Punchy thought leadership** (from Respond skill)
* **Structured SEO clarity** (from Blog skill)

---

### **CTA Examples**

* “Explore the platform”
